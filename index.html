FAQ

Is this telling me to post more?

No. Posting more can amplify a broken system. The diagnostic focuses on what happens after someone notices you.

Do I need to change my whole website?

Usually not. Most response issues come from one weak link: unclear next step, thin trust, or unnecessary friction.

Why call it “rizz” at all?

Because it is memorable. We use it as shorthand for response, not charisma. The goal is follow-through, not performance.

Will this work for local businesses?

Yes. “No inquiries from website” is one of the most common local-business problems. The mechanics are the same.
